# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Active traders and operators who use the terminal during focused market sessions. They need dense account, chart, order, and device state to remain legible while they monitor risk and act quickly.

## Product Purpose

This client provides the browser interface for a trading platform, centered on a dockable terminal with account context, live market views, device controls, commands, and operational feedback. Success means users can read state, compare signals, and execute intended actions with confidence under time pressure.

## Brand Personality

Quiet brokerage: restrained, precise, steady. The interface should feel composed and trustworthy rather than theatrical, with color reserved for action, state, and risk.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative finance dashboards, oversized marketing-style panels, low-contrast gray text, gratuitous neon effects, and visual treatments that make state colors feel like ornament instead of information.

## Design Principles

- Lead with scanability: dense surfaces should make the next relevant signal easy to find.
- Preserve trust through restraint: use accent color for current state, action, and focus, not decoration.
- Make risk unmistakable: success, warning, error, pending, and selected states need consistent semantics across themes.
- Favor durable defaults: tokens and components should support many theme packs without one-off overrides.
- Keep interaction familiar: standard controls, focus states, and panel behavior should stay predictable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target AAA where practical, with WCAG AA as the minimum floor for all interactive and body text. Favor strong focus indicators, reduced-motion-safe transitions, keyboard-reachable controls, and state cues that do not rely on color alone.
